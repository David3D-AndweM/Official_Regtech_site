About David Mwape

Welcome to my profile! I'm David Mwape, a visionary leader passionate about driving sustainability initiatives in Zambia. Currently, I serve as the President & Visionary of the Resilient Equity Green Tech Foundation, where we are dedicated to championing freshwater preservation and sustainability.

My journey began with eye-opening experiences alongside renowned organizations such as the Nature Conservancy and National Geographic Society. These experiences instilled in me a deep sense of responsibility towards preserving our environment, particularly our freshwater ecosystems.

Driven by my passion for conservation, I've embarked on various projects aimed at raising awareness and fostering community engagement. From developing strategic plans to spearheading fundraising efforts, I am committed to making a positive impact on our environment.

With a background in Software Engineering from African Leadership University, I leverage my technical expertise to innovate and implement sustainable solutions. My mission is to empower communities and pave the way for a greener, more sustainable future.

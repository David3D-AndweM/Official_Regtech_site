About Resilient Equity Green Tech Foundation

Hi there! I'm David Mwape, and I'm part of the team behind the Resilient Equity Green Tech Foundation here in Zambia. I'm passionate about doing our part to keep our environment healthy.

I wanted to share a bit about how I got into all of this. My journey started with some eye-opening experiences alongside the Nature Conservancy and National Geographic Society. They really showed me how important it is to take care of our water, and it made a big impact on me.

Since then, I've been diving into research and even created a story map to show what's happening with our freshwater and oceans. Now, I'm all about spreading the word on why it's crucial to protect our freshwater, especially here in Zambia where mining is putting people's access to clean water at risk.

Through down-to-earth campaigns and sharing stories that anyone can relate to, I want to make sure we're looking out for each other and our environment. If you're curious to learn more about what we're doing, I know I am a software engineering student with but my mission is very much about conservation.

As for now my mission is “To champion freshwater preservation in Zambia, leveraging research, community engagement, and storytelling to raise awareness about the critical importance of safeguarding our freshwater ecosystems. Through grassroots initiatives and advocacy efforts, we strive to empower communities to take proactive measures in preserving their water resources, ensuring sustainable access to clean water for present and future generations.”

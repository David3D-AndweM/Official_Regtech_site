Our Transformative Initiative

Resilient Equity Green Tech Foundation: Where each initiative is a whisper of change, inviting you to explore the extraordinary impact within our collective journey. Join us as we transform possibilities into realities.

JustUnity: Advocacy for all

The program promotes unity and advocacy for universal human rights, providing transformative practical and theoretical knowledge to participants, emphasizing the importance of justice and unity.

CURRENT

Digital Horizons: Empowering Through Digital Skills

We provide training and resources to help individuals gain digital and technological skills, enabling them to participate fully in the digital age.

CURRENT

Sustainable Forest Conservation and Community Empowerment Initiative

To address the issue of indiscriminate cutting down of trees for charcoal timber and caterpillars in Zambia, through a comprehensive and community-focused conservation initiative.

CURRENT

Community Support

We prioritize the needs of our community by advocating for marginalized groups, providing resources, and promoting social and economic justice.

CURRENT

Super League and Engagement Campaign

The Regy-Field Super League promotes community unity, professional football talent, and support for the Regtech Stars team, aiming for a brighter, more resilient community through football and community involvement.

CURRENT

Street Children Rehabilitation and Education Program

Streets to Dreams provides comprehensive support, shelter, education, and sustainable livelihoods to Chingola's street children, enabling them to lead self-sufficient lives.

Regtech Stars

As leaders, we are committed to the success of the RFSL initiative and its impact on the Chingola community. In this spirit, we have registered our own football team called REGTECH STARS. This team comprises a group of young, committed individuals with great potential who have inspired us in our journey. They require essential equipment, such as soccer boots and football jerseys, to participate fully.
